§ 01 — Philosophy

We believe a face cream should be as honest as the soil it came from — slow, traceable, and quietly effective.

Lambat

Each formula is steeped between 21 and 45 days. We do not rush — botanicals release their actives on their own schedule, not ours.

Terlacak

Eighty-seven percent of our ingredients are wild-crafted or grown within a 40-kilometre radius of our atelier in Munduk, Bali.

Berdaya

§ 03 — Kebun Kami

A small garden at 1,200 metres, in the clouds above Bali.

Our atelier sits on the northern ridge of Munduk — volcanic soil, cool mountain air, and ninety different cultivars in raised beds tended by the Kebun Sederhana cooperative.

The calendula petals dried in our serums were picked the same morning at 06:30, carried down by motorbike to our distillation room, and pressed before noon. Nothing in this house has travelled more than 40 km from where it grew.

White temple flowers from Ubud’s ceremonial trees, cold-enfleuraged into a creamy floral oil with a hint of vanilla.

Vetiver Root

Earthy roots from a Garut farm, distilled for 36 hours to capture the smoky depth that anchors our perfume base.

From the protected Sumba grove. We use only fallen heartwood — never felled trees — certified by Yayasan Cendana.

Mineral-rich grey clay collected at the foot of Mt. Batur. Draws out impurities without stripping the acid mantle.
