§ Bahan

Six botanicals.
One quiet formula.

Tekan dan tahan setiap bahan untuk membaca cerita panennya.

Rosehip Seed Oil — 68%

Rosa canina · cold-pressed · Munduk, Bali
The hero of the formula. Our rosehips are harvested in the cool November mornings, dried for nine days in shaded racks, then cold-pressed within twenty-four hours. Naturally high in trans-retinoic acid (a precursor to retinol) and omega-6, providing visible brightening without irritation.

Sea Buckthorn Berry — 12%

Hippophae rhamnoides · CO2-extracted
Sourced from a small organic farm in Yunnan. Carotenoid-rich, deeply nourishing, and the source of the serum’s warm honeyed colour.

Jojoba Oil — 9%

Simmondsia chinensis · organic
Closest botanical match to skin’s own sebum. Acts as a stabilising base, improves spreadability and supports the barrier.

Calendula Macerate — 6%

Calendula officinalis · sun-infused 21 days
Bright orange petals from our own beds, infused into jojoba for three full weeks. Calms reactive skin and adds a softening, sun-warmed quality.

Vitamin E (Natural) — 4%

Mixed tocopherols · non-GMO sunflower
Antioxidant protection and preservation. Keeps the serum fresh for twelve months from opening.

Frankincense Essential Oil — 1%

Boswellia sacra · steam-distilled · Sumbawa
Just enough to lend a meditative, faintly woody finish. Supports cellular regeneration and gives the serum its quiet, grounding aromatic signature.
§ Ritual Malam

A four-minute
evening ritual.

Apply as the last step of your evening — ideally with closed eyes, slowly.

Ritual Photography
01
Tenangkan

Begin with clean, slightly damp skin. Cup your hands over your face and take three slow breaths. Notice the temperature of your skin.

02
Tuang

Two to three drops into the palm. Warm the serum between your hands for five seconds — this releases the frankincense.

03
Tekan, jangan gosok

Press the serum into your skin with your palms, from the centre outward. Avoid rubbing. Pay extra attention to the orbital bone and jawline.

04
Diam sebentar

Wait sixty seconds before any cream. Let the serum settle. Let the day settle.
