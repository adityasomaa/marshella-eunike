§ Pertanyaan

The things we’re asked most often.

If your question isn’t answered here, write to us — we’ll add it to the page.

Berapa lama pengiriman?
Same-day dispatch for orders placed before 11:00 WITA. Bali deliveries arrive within 24-48 hours. Java 2-4 days. International 5-9 working days via DHL GoGreen.
Are the products safe during pregnancy?
Most of our range is pregnancy-friendly. The Rosehip Glow Serum (No.02) and Frankincense Eye Serum (No.11) contain essential oils — please consult your midwife. Email us for a full pregnancy-safe list.
Bagaimana cara mengembalikan kemasan?
Mail any empty amber bottle back to our Munduk atelier (we cover postage from anywhere in Indonesia) and receive Rp 25.000 credit toward your next purchase. We sterilise and refill in-house.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, to 38 countries via DHL GoGreen carbon-neutral shipping. Duties calculated at checkout. We do not ship to regions where natural cosmetics require additional registration.
Apakah saya bisa berkunjung ke kebun?
Yes — we host small group visits (max 8) on Saturdays at 10:00 from May to September. Includes a walking tour, a blending workshop, and tea with Ibu Made. Rp 850.000 per person. Book via the contact form.
My skin reacted — what should I do?
First, stop using it. Then email a photo to halo@terrabotanica.id. We’ll consult our in-house formulator and offer a full refund or a sample of an alternative. We take this seriously — ingredient reactions help us refine our patch-testing.
Are your formulas Halal certified?
Yes, all twelve formulas are Halal certified by MUI Indonesia since 2021. Certificate numbers are printed on every bottle base.
