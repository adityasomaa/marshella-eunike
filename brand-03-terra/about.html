§ Kisah Kami · Since 2018

A small apothecary, kept slowly & honestly, for eight years.

Terra Botanica began in 2018 as one woman, one steeping jar and a few rows of calendula in the cool air of Munduk. Eight years on, we are eighteen — tending six hectares of organic plots and a small distillation room above the village.

§ The Founder

Diah Pratiwi, and a question her grandmother used to ask.

“Why do we put on our skin what we wouldn’t feed our children?” The question came from her grandmother, a herbalist in Munduk who used calendula, turmeric and frangipani long before they were fashionable.

Diah trained as a chemical engineer in Bandung and worked in cosmetics manufacturing in Jakarta for seven years before returning home in 2017. She brought with her a formulary, a small grant, and a stubborn idea: that a serious botanical skincare line could be built without compromise — if it was built slowly enough.

The first batch of Verbena Cleansing Balm shipped in October 2018. Forty bottles. They sold out in three days.

§ Enam Pilar Botani

The six pillars of our apothecary.

Pilar · 01

Lokal & Terlacak

Eighty-seven percent of our botanicals are grown within a 40 km radius of our atelier. Each batch carries the harvest date and grower.

Pilar · 02

Maserasi Lambat

No steam acceleration, no chemical extraction. Botanicals are cold-infused for 21-45 days, exactly as our grandmothers did.

Pilar · 03

Tanpa Petrokimia

No silicones, parabens, phenoxyethanol, or synthetic fragrance. Ever. We publish our full formulary on every product page.

Pilar · 04

Kemasan Bisa Dikembalikan

Amber glass, aluminium caps, recycled paper labels. Return any empty for Rp 25.000 credit toward your next bottle.

Pilar · 05

Petani Diberdayakan

Our growers are co-owners. Eighteen women of the Kebun Sederhana cooperative share in fifteen percent of net revenue.

Pilar · 06

Iklim Net-Positif

We measure, offset, and now exceed our footprint. 38,420 trees planted since 2018 across Bali, Lombok and Sumba.

§ Peta Kebun

Six hectares, ninety cultivars.

Our farm is divided into seven sections by botanical family and sun exposure. Each plot is tended by two women of the cooperative.
