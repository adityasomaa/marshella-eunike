Lima konsep untuk
satu beauty brand
yang belum punya nama.

Halo. Setelah baca brief-mu di Threads, aku siapin lima arah desain yang berbeda — bukan satu mock-up template yang sama dipakai ulang. Tiap konsep adalah full site (7 halaman: home, shop, product, about, contact, cart, checkout) yang bisa kamu klik-klik dan rasain experiencenya sebelum kita pilih satu untuk dilanjut produksi. Tinggal pilih yang paling kamu suka — atau remix dari beberapa.

Yang kamu dapat di setiap konsep.

Tiap brand bukan sekedar mock-up — semua halaman fully clickable, cart functional, checkout multi-step. Kamu bisa rasain end-to-end customer journey sebelum approve.

01
Home Page
Hero with motion BG, featured products, brand story sections, journal teaser, newsletter.
02
Shop / Catalog
12+ produk per brand, filter sidebar (kategori, harga, skin type), sort dropdown, hover-to-reveal.
03
Product Detail
Image gallery, ingredients, how-to, reviews, "complete the routine" upsell, add-to-bag drawer.
04
About / Brand
Founder story, atelier visuals, values, animated counters, sustainability/certifications.
05
Contact
Inquiry form, store locations, FAQ accordion, social handles, wholesale CTA.
06
Cart Drawer + Page
Slide-in drawer, qty steppers, free-gift progress, promo code, gift-wrap/engraving options.
07
Checkout Flow
Multi-step (shipping → payment → review), order summary sidebar, IDR currency, payment toggles.
+
Motion Background
Setiap brand punya BG canvas yang unik, mouse-reactive, performant 60fps di desktop & mobile.

Tech stack.

FRAMEWORK 01
Bootstrap 5.3
Grid sistem, utility class, responsive breakpoints. Konsisten dengan request klien — production-ready & well-documented.
CMS / COMMERCE
Drupal 10 + Commerce
Backend: product catalog, cart, checkout, order management, customer accounts. Demo ini frontend-only mock; production akan wired ke Drupal API.
MOTION
GSAP 3.12 + Canvas
ScrollTrigger untuk reveals, custom Canvas requestAnimationFrame untuk interactive BG per brand.
DEPLOY
Vercel · GitHub CI
Repo: github.com/adityasomaa/marshella-eunike — auto-deploy preview tiap push, production di Vercel edge network.
