Composition

Inside the bottle.

Ingredients

A short, slow list. Pressed cold where possible, fermented for fourteen days, then rested.

Hero notes — Saccharomyces ferment filtrate · tamanu seed oil (Banda Aceh) · 24-karat gold leaf · niacinamide 4% · bakuchiol 0.5% · centella asiatica from Bali highlands · panthenol B5.

Base — squalane (olive-derived) · jojoba esters · damask rose hydrosol · vitamin E.

Free from: synthetic fragrance, parabens, sulfates, mineral oil, silicones. Vegan, cruelty-free, never tested.

How to use · Le ritual

Pour three drops into the palm of the non-dominant hand at the end of the day. Warm gently. Press — never rub — into freshly cleansed skin, beginning at the throat and rising toward the temples.

Allow ninety seconds before sealing with the Gold Leaf Crème. Use nightly. Within twenty-eight nights, the skin begins to remember itself.

Sourcing & the atelier

Botanicals sourced from family growers in Banda Aceh, Mount Bromo, and the Sabah highlands. Glass is mouth-blown in Yogyakarta. The cap is hand-tightened, every bottle, in our Jakarta atelier.

Sustainability promise

Refillable architecture. Return the empty bottle to any AURA NOIR atelier and receive 15% off your refill. Recycled cartons, soy-based inks, no plastic shrink wrap.

Edition certificate

Each bottle ships with a numbered certificate of authenticity, signed in golden ink by the composer of the batch. Edition 042 was pressed on the 12th of April, MMXXVI, under a waxing moon.
