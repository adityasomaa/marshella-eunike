Pertanyaan Umum

Soft answers, quietly given.

If your question is not below, write to us. We answer everything, by hand, within forty-eight hours.

Write to concierge
How long does shipping take across Indonesia?

Within Jakarta, same-day before 14:00. Elsewhere on Java, 1—2 business days. Bali, Sumatra, and beyond, 3—5 business days. International EMS, 7—14 days. Every order ships in a hand-tied carton with a complimentary set of samples.

Apakah produk AURA NOIR halal?

Ya. Every formula is vegan, alcohol-denat-free in skincare, and certified halal by LPPOM MUI. Our fragrances are pressed with botanical alcohols certified for cosmetic use.

Can I return or refill an empty bottle?

Of course. Return any empty AURA NOIR bottle to one of our three ateliers (or post it back with our prepaid label) and receive 15% off your refill. The bottle goes back to Yogyakarta, where it is rinsed, sterilized, and re-poured.

Do you offer private gifting and engraving?

Yes. Engraving up to fourteen characters is complimentary on any 30ml or larger bottle. Private gift consultations are available with our concierge by appointment, including wax-sealed letters, calligraphy on the carton, and curated discovery sets.

Are the formulas safe during pregnancy?

Most of our skincare is pregnancy-friendly, but some editions contain bakuchiol or essential oils that we recommend discussing with your physician first. The product detail page lists pregnancy guidance for every edition.

How do I become a stockist?

Write to us at wholesale@auranoir.id with your store, location, and a short note about why AURA NOIR feels at home with you. We work with a small, curated set of partners.
