Le Rituel

A four-step nightly ceremony.

Beauty, at AURA NOIR, is not a transaction — it is a return. A slow undoing of the day. A small, golden pause before sleep.

Cleanse

Begin with the Midnight Cleansing Oil. Warm between palms. Inhale the smoked vanilla, then dissolve the day from the skin in slow circles.

Tone

A single press of the Onyx Toning Mist. Three breaths. Let the rose-water and amber resin settle into the bare canvas of the face.

Treat

Three drops of Noir Élixir Serum. Pressed, never rubbed, into temple, throat, décolletage. The skin remembers what is given slowly.

Seal

Finish with the Gold Leaf Crème. A whisper of 24-karat shimmer. Sleep arrives, gilded, and the morning finds you new.

Maison Notes

Each batch, signed by the hand that pressed it.

Our serums rest for forty-two days in amber glass before they leave the maison. Every bottle is numbered. Every cap, hand-tightened. We make exactly as much as we can make slowly — never one drop more.

  • Vegan formulas, cruelty-free across the entire atelier
  • Glass bottles, refillable architecture, recycled cartons
  • 100% Indonesian-sourced botanicals where the climate permits
  • Each edition limited; once released, never restocked
